How To Play

Press 'R' to toggle between ready and unready. When all players in the room are ready, the game will start.Fight an endless onslaught of enemies in melee combat.

1. Click the left mouse button to attack in the direction of the mouse from your player.

2. Move using "WASD" or arrow keys to avoid enemies

3. Hit enemies with your attack to gain points, and avoid being hit by enemies or you will lose health. When you are all out of health you will die.

You have 100 points of health that will start to drain once an enemy touches you. Enemies only need to be hit once to kill them. Kill as many as you can before you and your friends die!
